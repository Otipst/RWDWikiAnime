"Four Best Isekai Anime Recommendations in Winter 2023"

Because some isekai anime this year have often made it to the top playlist, one of them being on the Bstation channel.
Isekai anime tells the story of someone who is transported to another world. This fantasy adventure captivates viewers and allows them to enjoy the film.
For those who are still unsure about which isekai anime to watch, here are 4 best isekai anime recommendations for Winter 2023.










1. The Eminence in Shadow (Shadow Garden)

Who wouldn't be interested in a character who becomes a hero behind the scenes?
This anime follows the adventures of Cid Kagenou, who gets a second chance at life in a magical world after failing to achieve his goals in modern Japan.
Cid dreams of becoming a hero, but in an unconventional way. He chooses to operate from the shadows while leading a secret organization called Shadow Garden.
Together with the members of his organization, they strive to bring down a corrupt sect.




2. Isekai Ojisan

This anime features the unique story of two main characters: Yousuke Shibazaki, an old man, and his nephew, Takafumi Takaoka.
After being in a coma for 17 years, Yousuke suddenly wakes up and discovers that he can speak in a strange language and possesses magical powers.
At first, Takafumi thinks his uncle has lost his sanity, but he later realizes that his uncle has actually been transported to an isekai world. Together, they embark on exciting adventures in this fantasy world.




3. Tondemo Skill de Isekai Hourou Meshi

Mukouda Tsuyoshi and three other heroes are unexpectedly summoned to a fantasy world with a mission to save the kingdom from a threat.
However, Mukouda himself doesn't possess strong combat abilities like the others. Instead, he has a unique skill called "Food Menu."
With this skill, Mukouda can create delicious dishes and acquire modern Japanese ingredients through the shops he visits.
This anime presents an interesting combination of cooking skills and epic adventures in a fantasy world.




4. Isekai Nonbiri

Machio Hiraku dies from illness at the age of 39, but God gives him a second chance in another world.
During the meeting with God, Hiraku requests good health, a peaceful life, the ability to understand the local language, and his final dream of becoming a farmer.
This anime depicts Hiraku's peaceful and tranquil life in the new world, where he lives as a happy farmer.
The story provides a refreshing touch by focusing on the simple daily life and the happiness that can be found in simple activities.




Those are the four best isekai anime recommendations for Winter 2023, with engaging and exciting stories. Enjoy watching!
